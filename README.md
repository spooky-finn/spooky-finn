The era of de-globalization is here, and it is quieter than I expected.

I’ve spent the last six years in the code, watching the "future of finance" devolve into a series of polite apologies for "rug pulls" and VC exits. It’s "just business," they say. But the sadness of the industry is that we were given a profound gift: the right to own our past lives—our capital.

Money is just stored time, and Satoshi gave us the right to keep it solely within our minds. Most of the world will never realize this right, and the industry seems content to let it slide into the noise of marketing. I build backend infrastructure and sovereign tools not because I’m an optimist, but because I know the value of what’s being lost.
